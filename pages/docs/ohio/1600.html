1600 / 600 (1600EBK / 600 BloccBaby Crips)

Category: Ohio Criminal Gangs • Date: 10/14/2025

Overview

1600EBK, also known as 600 BloccBaby Crips, is a street organization based in the Wedgewood neighborhood of Columbus, Ohio. Emerging as an offshoot of BL-800, the group unites members from various Crip factions, including the Rolling 60's Crips and BackSt Crips, under a unified identity.

Background

Founded between 2019 and early 2020 by DD (Damari), 1600EBK formed as a response to the dynamics within the local gang politics. The group's establishment and presence within the Wedgewood area exploded in late 2021-2022 after Damari was shot and killed.

As of 2025, 1600EBK maintains a presence in several neighborhoods and cities such as Cleveland, Ohio, Columbus, Ohio, Portsmouth, Ohio, Dayton, Ohio, and has gained a small presence in the Midwest Chicago region connected with Chicago sets such as the BD's (Black Disciples), and continues to expand its membership base. 1600 emphasizes unity among members and fostering connections across formerly separate Crip factions.
In Chicago, 1600 connected/linked sets go by the names of 16Gang, 1600 & Forever16. They are based in the Parkway Gardens complex, also known as OBlock, made famous by popular drill artist King Von.
