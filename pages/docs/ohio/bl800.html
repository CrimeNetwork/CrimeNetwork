BL-800 Gang (Columbus, OH)

Category: Real-Life Gangs • Date: 10/14/2025

Overview

BL-800 is a notorious gang operating in Columbus, OH. They have been involved in a variety of criminal activities over the years and have established influence in multiple neighborhoods.

Background

The gang emerged in the early 2000s and quickly expanded its presence across the city. Local authorities have noted their organized structure and alliances with other regional gangs.

BL-800 members are known to engage in street-level crimes as well as larger, coordinated operations. Their activity has drawn significant law enforcement attention.

Investigation / Details

Law enforcement reports have documented arrests, confiscations, and ongoing investigations into BL-800 operations. Federal and local agencies continue to monitor gang activity in Columbus.

Intelligence reports also suggest the gang's involvement in trafficking, organized theft, and other criminal networks extending beyond city limits.

  • Founded: Early 2000s, Columbus, OH
  • Primary Territory: Columbus neighborhoods
  • Known Activities: Drug trafficking, organized theft
  • Law Enforcement Attention: High
