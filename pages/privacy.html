Privacy Policy for Crime Network

Effective Date: October 13, 2025

Last Edited: October 13, 2025

1. Who We Are

Crime Network ("we," "us," or "our") is a media and cybersecurity platform dedicated to investigating real-world and digital crimes while helping you stay safe online. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your privacy.

2. CrimeVPN – Zero-Logs, Guaranteed

When you use CrimeVPN, we do NOT log anything. No browsing history, no connection timestamps, no IP addresses—nothing. Your activity while connected to CrimeVPN is completely private and anonymous.

3. Our Websites

When you visit Crime Network websites, we may collect minimal data like:

  • Your IP address
  • Device type and browser
  • Pages you visit

This data is used ONLY to keep our sites running smoothly, improve performance, and protect against abuse.

4. How We Use Your Info

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To respond to inquiries or support requests
  • To analyze site performance and security

5. Sharing Your Info

We do NOT sell or trade your personal data. We may share information:

  • With trusted service providers to run our services
  • When required by law or to protect safety, rights, or property

6. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies and analytics on our websites to improve your experience. You can control or disable cookies in your browser.

7. Security

We take strong measures to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we do everything reasonable to keep your information safe.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Your Rights

  • Access or delete your data
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Contact us with questions about your privacy

10. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time, without notice or obligation to inform users. Using our services after changes means you accept the updated terms.
